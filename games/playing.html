Priconne

This is just an auto-battle game with high production quality. I bought 1 month package so I'm no longer f2p.

Usually I play for 20 minutes in the morning and another 20 minutes before bed. It's a low commitment game which respect my free times.

This is my first time playing Yakuza serie. The story is mature which is a great departure from the usual fantasy setup.

I don't particularly enjoy the dungeon sections as I feel they drag out too long.
